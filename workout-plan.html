Non-Negotiables
01

Consistency over intensity

3 sessions every week beats 5 for 2 weeks then burnout. The habit is the foundation.

02

Progressive overload is everything

Add 1–2 reps or small weight every week. Same weight after 4 weeks = no growth. Track every lift.

03

Form first, always

First 2 weeks: lighter than you think. Correct movement patterns prevent injuries that set you back months.

04

Protein every single day

Gym without protein is noise. 100–110g daily minimum. Non-negotiable.

05

Fix your sleep timing

Same wake-up daily. Irregular sleep tanks testosterone and growth hormone — critical at 25.

06

Never skip legs

Biggest muscle group. Squats and deadlifts release the most anabolic hormones. Skipping legs slows upper body gains too.

07

Warm up every session

5–7 min treadmill or light cycling before lifting. Cold muscles + heavy compounds = injury.

08

Book the blood retest

Vitamin D and B12 deficiencies sabotage energy and recovery. Don't train hard while still deficient.
