Non-Negotiables
01

Consistency over intensity

3 sessions every week beats 5 sessions for 2 weeks then burnout. The habit is the foundation.

02

Progressive overload is everything

Add 1–2 reps or small weight increment weekly. Same weight after 4 weeks = no growth. Track every lift.

03

Form first, always

First 2 weeks: go lighter than you think. Correct movement patterns prevent injuries that set you back months.

04

Protein every single day

Gym without protein is noise. 100–110g daily minimum. Missing even one day sets muscle protein synthesis back.

05

Fix your sleep timing

Same wake-up time daily including weekends. Irregular sleep tanks testosterone and growth hormone — both critical at 25.

06

Never skip legs

Legs are your biggest muscle group. Squats and deadlifts release the most anabolic hormones. Skipping legs slows upper body gains too.

07

Warm up every session

5–7 minutes treadmill walk or light cycling before lifting. Cold muscles + heavy compound lifts = injury.

08

Get the blood retest done

Vitamin D and B12 deficiencies sabotage energy, mood, and recovery. Don't train hard while still potentially deficient. Book it this week.
